Get To Know Our Campus

ESMAD


The Degree in Information Technologies and Systems for the Web, TSIW, taught at the Escola Superior de Media Artes e Design, ESMAD,

on Campus 2 of the Porto Polytechnic Institute, one of the most prestigious arts and design schools in Portugal.

ESMAD's TSIW Degree has a strong tradition in teaching and research in various areas of the arts,

meaning demand far exceeds supply, from students across the country.

Web Tecnologies


The Degree in Information Technologies and Systems for the Web, TSIW, assumes great importance and influence in the Portuguese job market,

technological and digital landscape and contributes to the competitiveness of the Portuguese economy.

The Degree in Information Technologies and Systems for the Web, TSIW, is based on offering comprehensive training

and a curricular program constantly updated with the latest market trends in web technologies.

MODERN INFRASTRUCTURES


The degree in Information Technologies and Systems for the Web, TSIW,

has modern infrastructure and facilities available to students.

These represent enormous added value in teaching Information Technologies and Systems for the Web,

in areas such as web design, programming, front-end, back-end, UI and UX.

HIGH EMPLOYABILITY


For those looking for a career in the web development area, the degree in Information Technologies and Systems for the Web

is an excellent option with an employability rate very close to 100%.

The Degree in Information Technologies and Systems for the Web contributed to this employability rate

by offering a solid and comprehensive training, based on partnerships with several leading companies in the area.
